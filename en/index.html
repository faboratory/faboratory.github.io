Faboratory is a digital fabrication development platform, in residency at Hangar (Barcelona).


It's activity is a mix of research, project development (internal and external) and communication (lectures, workshops, research documentation).


Faboratory aims to become: a virtual and physical reference space, services, and specially a community of users and contributors.


Open doors each thursday from 18:00 to 22:00, unless notified (web/twitter).
